# Research Data: The Effect of the Remunicipalization of Urban Water on Water Prices and Consumption
This repository contains the data files for "The Effect of the Remunicipalization of Urban Water on Water Prices and Consumption"

## Variable names

- **id**: dataset-level municipality identifier
- **Idescat_code**: identifier by the Statistical Institute of Catalonia
**municipality**: Standardized municipality name
**year**: Year of observation
**group**: Year when municipality remunicipalized urban water
**group_threat**: Year when service contract expired without remunicipalization
**treat_ever**: Indicator for being eventually treated
**water_use**: Use of water per capita, in $m^3$
**water_price**: Price of water in EUR
**population**: Population in reference year
**shr_left_2011:** vote share of center-left parties in 2011
**shr_left_2015:** vote share of center-left parties in 2011
**shr_left_2016:** vote share of center-left parties in 2011
**shr_14oryounger_2013**: Share of population aged 14 or younger in 2013
**shr_65orolder_2013**: Share of population aged 65 or older in 2013
**shr_14oryounger_2016:** Share of population aged 14 or younger in 2016
**shr_65orolder_2016:** Share of population aged 65 or older in 2016
**shr_tertiaryedu_2021:** Share of population with tertiary education in 2016
**shared_basins:** Indicator for municipalities sharing basins of river systems with other autonomous communities 
**index_hydrological_drought:** Period-weighted average drought indicator for the Catalan drought between 2021 and 2023
